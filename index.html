The Wynwood Miami Art district

Wynwood is in the North of Miami near the Design District (fashion district), 5 minutes from downtown, and just 17 minutes from of South Beach and the ocean.

Five years ago, this district was a poorly attended industrial area made up of industrial warehouses. In recent years, with the help of the city and developers, many "street artists" known worldwide have made the name of "Street-Art" (or Art-Urban) in the United States and around the world. Today, it is a unique place of its kind. Many multicolored murals decorate all the walls of the neighborhood and new restaurants, bars, art galleries and trendy shops are emerging. Today there are 70 art galleries and 20 restaurants.

The free event, "Art Walk", attracts an impressive number of residents and tourists every second Saturday of the month. Artists exhibit and paint their works live in a fun atmosphere. New murals are added regularly and most of them are renewed every year during the Art Basel event in December.
